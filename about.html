AMHARIC DEPARTMENT

The Amharic Department at the Ghana Accra Missionary Training Center provides essential language training for missionaries preparing to serve in Ethiopia Addis-Ababa Mission regions. With a focus on fostering linguistic proficiency and cultural understanding, our dedicated instructors facilitate immersive learning experiences. Through interactive sessions and language drills, missionaries gain the necessary skills to communicate effectively, ensuring a meaningful connection with the Amharic-speaking communities they'll serve.

ESL DEPARTMENT

Our ESL (English as a Second Language) Department plays a pivotal role in preparing missionaries for their service in diverse linguistic environments. Committed to facilitating language acquisition, our department offers comprehensive English language training tailored to equip missionaries with the tools for effective communication and cultural integration. Through dynamic lessons and immersive experiences, we empower individuals to confidently navigate English-speaking communities during their missions, fostering meaningful connections and impactful interactions.

FRENCH DEPARTMENT

Our French Department stands as a cornerstone in preparing missionaries for service in French-speaking regions worldwide. Through a curriculum designed to instill linguistic proficiency and cultural appreciation, our dedicated instructors guide students in mastering the French language. Immersive lessons and interactive experiences enable missionaries to navigate conversations, comprehend local nuances, and connect meaningfully with French-speaking communities. Equipped with language fluency and a deeper understanding of the culture, missionaries are poised to share their message of faith and compassion effectively, fostering enduring relationships in their service areas.

SWAHILI DEPARTMENT

Our Swahili Department serves as a vital platform for missionaries preparing to serve in Swahili-speaking regions.
